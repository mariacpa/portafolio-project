Workshops

Workshops are a great way to transfer knowledge and raise the level of awareness towards health and nutrition. I invite you join and broaden your horizons and most importantly to appreciate and enjoy the benefits of eating while keeping in mind your personal needs, to learn how you can easily take care of yourself and get tools to nurture your life.

At home

The workshops are held in home kitchens according to the format and number of participants. We can focus on a specific medical problem, ingredient or cooking technique. Or it can be an interesting experiential activity.

Lectures

I frequently participate in forums and conversations held in different spaces around the country. You are welcome to come and join the conversation about healthy nutrition vs personalized nutrition, the connection between food and medical problems, and many more. Follow me in Facebook to get the latest events and dates.
