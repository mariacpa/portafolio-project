Adjusted Nutrition

There is a common mistake in believing that there are good and bad foods in general. The right approach is understanding that there are right foods for each person and this treatment has the purpose of discovering which are the perfect fit to your needs.

The Diagnostic

We will start with a diagnostic meeting where I will use Chinese Medicine techniques to understand your condition and match the foods and cooking techniques that will contribute to improve your health. By the end of this meeting, you will receive a list of food adjusted to your needs.

The Menu

We will have our next meeting three days after. Here we wil build a menu based on the list you received in the first meeting. The menu will be adapted to your needs, preferences, and your lifestyle.

Follow-ups

We will have two follow-up meetings after two to three weeks in which we will monitor your progress, and adjust the diagnosis and nutrition.
